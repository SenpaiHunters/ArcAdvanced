Arc.hive

A Click Through History

Since the dawn

Arc.hive began as a project focused on preserving Arc's legacy. Our team has invested significant time and effort in documenting Arc's versioning system. As a result, we have curated a collection of different Arc versions, including unreleased ones. Our goal is to honour Arc's contributions by offering a valuable resource that encourages users to explore its history and find inspiration for the future.

Arc Advanced

With the discovery of the mechanics of Arc's file system, we've devised a programme that allows users to safely modify Arc's file system and enable features that are locked behind feature flags. All done in a safe and reversible way. This programme is strictly opt-in and offers support in our Discord server.
